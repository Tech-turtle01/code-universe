ABOUT US

PASTRY PALACE befitting our exclusive and indulgent range of offerings that include brownies, cakes, desserts, chocolates, breads and savouries. From our humble beginnings in 2004, when the pastry palace store opened its doors at the iconic Cusrow Baug at Colaba Causeway (Mumbai), we've grown to become a Pan-India store. Our mission is to spread happiness by serving smiles on a plate, and this journey continues as we open Theobroma patisseries across the country.
